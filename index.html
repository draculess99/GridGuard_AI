The operational problem

Forecasts alone are not enough.

Grid operators need more than a predicted number. They need evidence, policy context, scenario awareness, and a governed path from insight to action.

GridGuard closes the decision gap.

It combines machine learning with deterministic controls, explainable evidence, and operator review so every recommendation can be challenged, understood, and audited.

Responsible AI governance

Designed to assist—not replace—the grid operator.

GridGuard separates prediction, policy, recommendation, and authority so no model output becomes an operational action without accountable human review.
